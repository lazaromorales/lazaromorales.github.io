LA Tekkerz

A simple website showcasing the work and futbol private lessons offered by LA Tekkerz

Scroll to Details

The Abstract

LA Tekkerz needed to showcase their business and what they offer in their futbol private trainings. They needed an online presence and website for interested clients to be able to visit.

The Product

I started by designing a logo on Adobe Illustrator to capture the business's brand and color palette. Then I began creating a simple design to capture the details of every image, to showcase social media platforms such as Instagram, and to capture interested leads through deployment of Netlify which captures client emails.
